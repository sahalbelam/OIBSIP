Tribute to Khabib Nurmagomedov

The Eagle

Khabib "The Eagle" Nurmagomedov is a retired mixed martial artist, widely regarded as one of the greatest fighters in the history of MMA.

He was born on September 20, 1988, in Sildi, Dagestan ASSR, Russian SFSR, Soviet Union. Khabib comes from a family with a long history of wrestling, with his father Abdulmanap Nurmagomedov being his coach and mentor.

During his career, Khabib amassed an undefeated record of 29 wins and 0 losses, with notable victories over Conor McGregor, Dustin Poirier, and Rafael dos Anjos. He was known for his grappling prowess and relentless pressure inside the octagon.

Outside of fighting, Khabib is known for his humility, respect, and strong connection to his Dagestani roots. He has inspired many with his dedication to his craft and his commitment to his family and community.

"I don't fight for the money. I fight for my legacy. I fight for history. I fight for my people." - Khabib Nurmagomedov

- Khabib Nurmagomedov
